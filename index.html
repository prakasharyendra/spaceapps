Global Event Overview
NASA Space Apps Challenge is a global collaboration challenge conducted by NASA in 75+ countries and with 18,000+ participants. The Space Apps challenge brings together ideas from students, designers, startups, working professionals, research scholars and domain experts on one common platform.

Organised by NASA's Office of the Chief Information Officer, #spaceapps has made it to the list of World's Largest Hackathon with solution built on top of Open Data provided by NASA.
